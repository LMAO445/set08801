Geography

Located in South America, Argentina is the 8th largest country in the world.

It has a diverse climate, from subtropical in the north to cold Antarctic conditions in the south.

Culture & People

Argentinians are known for their passion for football (soccer), tango dance, and mate (herbal tea).

The country has a strong European influence, especially from Spain and Italy.

Economy

Argentina is one of the world's largest producers of beef, soybeans, and wine (especially from Mendoza).

The economy has had ups and downs, with inflation and financial crises being major challenges.
