Geography

Belgium is bordered by France, Germany, the Netherlands, and Luxembourg.

Capital City is Brussels. The political center of the European Union and NATO.

Culture & People

Belgium has produced world-famous painters like René Magritte and Peter Paul Rubens.

Festivals & Traditions:

Carnival of Binche_A UNESCO-recognized festival with masked performers.

Tomorrowland_One of the world’s biggest electronic music festivals

Economy

Strong Economy: One of the most developed countries in Europe.

Major Industries

Finance & Banking_Brussels is a financial hub.

Technology & Pharmaceuticals_Advanced research and medical innovations.

Diamonds_Antwerp is the world’s leading diamond trading center.
