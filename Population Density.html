Population Density

Albania 98 people/km²

Australia 3 people/km²

Belgium 383 people/km²

Argentina 17 people/km²

Belgium has the highest population density due to its small land area and highly urbanized regions.

Australia has the lowest population density because of its vast desert areas (outback), with most people living in coastal cities.

Albania and Argentina fall in between, with Argentina having a much lower density due to its large land area.
